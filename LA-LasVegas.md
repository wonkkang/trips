## Day 1 - Saturday: Arrival + Santa Monica

### Option A (low stress, best first day)
- Check in and reset
- Santa Monica Pier + Pacific Park
- Third Street Promenade dinner
- Beach sunset walk

### Option B (a little more energy)
- Santa Monica Pier
- Venice Boardwalk + Venice Canals
- Back to Santa Monica for dinner

### Core Stops
1. **Santa Monica Pier**
   - **Why it stands out:** This is classic Southern California in one frame: ocean air, ferris wheel, arcade energy, and sunset all at once.
   - **Why you'll like it:** It is easy fun with no learning curve after travel day. Teens and college-age kids can split off and still stay close.
   - Estimated time: 2-3 hours
   - Maps: <a href="https://maps.google.com/?q=Santa+Monica+Pier" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Open in Google Maps</a>

2. **Third Street Promenade**
   - **Why it stands out:** Lively pedestrian district with street performers and flexible food options.
   - **Why you'll like it:** Everyone can pick their own vibe after a long flight/drive day.
   - Estimated time: 1.5-2 hours
   - Maps: <a href="https://maps.google.com/?q=Third+Street+Promenade+Santa+Monica" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Open in Google Maps</a>
   - Link: <a href="https://www.santamonica.com/what-to-do/third-street-promenade/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Official Site</a>

## Day 4 Decision Guide: Universal Studios Hollywood vs Six Flags (and Orlando comparison)

### Universal Orlando vs Universal Hollywood: Are They Similar?
**Yes, similar enough to feel repetitive for repeat Orlando visitors**:
- Both have **Wizarding World** experiences in their resort offerings.
- Both have **SUPER NINTENDO WORLD** offerings in 2026-era products.
- Both use major movie/game IP immersion as a core draw.

**What is uniquely LA at Universal Hollywood**:
- The **World-Famous Studio Tour** and working backlot context is the best LA-specific differentiator.
- Hollywood is a compact, single-park day; Orlando is a multi-park vacation ecosystem.

### Six Flags vs Universal Hollywood (Practical Comparison)
| Decision Factor | Six Flags Magic Mountain | Universal Studios Hollywood |
|---|---|---|
| Thrill intensity | Higher, coaster-centric all day | Mixed thrills + simulators + themed rides |
| Novelty vs your Orlando history | Higher novelty | Lower novelty (more IP overlap) |
| Film immersion | Lower inside the park | Higher (Studio Tour + movie theming) |
| Best fit for teens/college-age thrill seekers | Strong | Moderate to strong |
| Day structure | Pure ride-chasing | More balanced theme/show day |
| Best use of this trip | **Recommended** | Alternate if family votes for movie focus |
